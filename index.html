About The Event

Digital Era Summit 2026 is a flagship technology event organized at Silver Oak University, bringing together innovators, developers, researchers, and future tech leaders under one roof.

This event is designed to explore emerging domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Startup Innovation. Attendees will gain real-world insights through keynote sessions, technical showcases, live demonstrations, and collaborative networking opportunities.

Hosted at Aryabatta Hall, the summit aims to inspire students to step confidently into the next generation of digital transformation and technological advancement.
